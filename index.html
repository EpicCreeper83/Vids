Speed, Time & Distance



Speed, distance & time are key things to know especially if you are a an aircraft controller because you need to know where all these planes are going and when they will be there. To do this they use 3 formulas 1. Distance = Speed x Time 2. Time = Distance/speed 3. Speed = Distance/Time. Using these three formulas they can then measure where the plane will be according to its speed and the time taken. They can also then check how long it's taken and when it should reach its destination. This prevents lots of accidents.
